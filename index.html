The World's Healthcare System is Broken

Affordability, Accesibility and Authenticity are the three pillars of successful medical treatment. Unfortunately, they are rarely present in developing nations.

Accessibility 

At present, 400 million people do not have access to basic medical treatment. 

Affordability 

Pharmaceutical companies make
billions in profits while pricing out
most of those in developing nations. 

Authenticity

On average, 10% of medicines in developing countries are counterfeited resulting in millions of deaths per year.

Our Solution

End-to-end supply chain tracking from manufacturer through to patient,
fully decentralised using the latest in blockchain and artficial Intelligence technology.

Pharmaceutical Companies

By allowing pharmaceutical companies the ability to track and trace their products across the supply chain, we add security and maximise their revenues.

Locals


We will give back directly to local communities by creating jobs in providing secure delivery of medicines to patients.

Patients


Patients can interact with our simple SMS-based interface to request medicines. We will ensure that these remain affordable.

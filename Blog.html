Winners of the African Healthcare Hackathon 2018

Sunday 25th February 2018

We entered the African Healthcare Hackathon in February of this year with not much more than a vision and a drive to bring it one step closer to reality. With the support we received from our mentors over the weekend from the Kopfadeyemi Foundation we were able to build a small prototype and ironed out some key business dilemmas and communicate our vision to a panel of experts. In winning, we obtained a place on the African Healthcare Hackathon incubator and are very excited to be taking things further.
